Teletubbies

Characters Description

Tinky-Winky 		Tinky-winky is the purple teletubby, he is also the oldest among his friends and also the tallest
He is very kind and gentle

Dipsy 		Dipsy is the green teletubby, he has an antenna that refers to dipstick because of his name
He is 8 foot tall and is known for being stubborn, sarcastic and contemptuous

Laa-laa 		Laa-laa is the yellow teletubby, she has a curly antenna on her head
She is very girly and likes playing with her ball and doing ballet

Po 		Po is the red teletubby, she has a circle-shaped antenna on her head
She can speak English as well as Cantonese

Sun Baby		 The Baby Sun is the sun of Teletubbyland that shines in the sky and makes baby noises. Her face is live-action and the sun is performed by Jess Smith when she was 9 months old. This sun giggles and laughs being excited during any Magical Event or watching the Teletubbies do funny and silly things.
